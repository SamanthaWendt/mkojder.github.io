FAQ

Q: Do I have to be good to join?
A: No, we accept all levels of play!

Q: How do I sign up for tournaments?
A: We will send out an email 2-3 weeks before a tournament with the deadline to sign up. All you do is reply to that email.

Q: How long are tournaments?
A: Tournaments are on Saturdays. If it’s far like Houston, then we will leave Friday night and come back Saturday. If it’s closer like Waco, then we’ll drive up Saturday morning and come back that night.
